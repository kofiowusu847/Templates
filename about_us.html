Who we are

Established in 1991, GMT Surveys is professional and outstanding company, engaged in providing reliable land surveying and physical planning services across the length and breadth of Ghana. Our portfolio of services include Cadastral Surveys, Topographic Surveys, Construction Surveys, GPS Point Positioning, Disputed Boundary Surveys, Mapping and Spatial planning. Our dedicated survey teams provide geographically referenced information to technical stakeholders including Architects, Urban Planners, Civil Engineers, Soil Engineers and Hydrologists. We take pride in our competence to deliver accurate results to our clients. GMT Surveys apply modern methodologies, advanced surveying equipment and software in rendering land surveying services. As a devoted team, we ensure all services are delivered in a professional manner to suit the needs of our patrons. Hence, gaining the needed trust from various stakeholders including government agencies, local authorities, communities and individual project owners.

What means to us

Mission

In the wake of rapid urbanization, we find it necessary to play a major role in sustainable development by providing accurate and reliable geographic information to our clients in order to save them time and money.

Technical Staff

We have a highly motivated and dynamic team of Geomatic Engineers and Planners with many years of field experience. The technical team is responsible for gathering field data, processing the data and preparing plans for clients. They are also responsible for ensuring best practices are adhered to and quality assurance goals sustained.

Company's CEO

George M. Eto is a seasoned Geodetic Engineer and has over thirty years of experience in various aspects of Geodetic Engineering.His area of specialization includes, Ground Control Survey, Road survey, Topographic Survey, Route Location, Utility surveys. Mr. Eto has in-depth knowledge also in Demarcation and Survey of disputed boundaries, Mining and Installation of factory machinery, Construction and Farm Surveys. He has served as a mining consultant to numerous mining companies including Tarkwa Gold Mines Ltd., Prestea Gold Mines, Sankofa Gold Mines and Bonte Gold Mines Ltd. He currently serves as a consultant to Asante Hene's Lands Secretariat and Premium Foods Limited.
